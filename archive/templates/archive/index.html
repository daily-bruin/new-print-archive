Daily Bruin | Print Archive

The Daily Bruin has served the UCLA community since 1919, and its predecessors date back to 1912. On this site you can read every issue of the Daily Bruin from 1915 to present. Digital archives of the Daily Bruin from 1915 to 2002 on this site were made possible by the UCLA Library. More recent issues were compiled from Daily Bruin archives. Bound archives of the Daily Bruin are also available upon request. Please call (310) 825-9898 to schedule an appointment to access the bound archives.
